Centre for Orthopedic Care

The department treats bone and joints related issues under two main sections

Orthopedics : Treats issues related to bone, joints, ligament, tendons etc.

Spine : Treats issues related to the backbone, i.e. spine. Spine, which is known as backbone in common language makes us stand straight, bend etc. The posture of our body is mainly maintained by spine. It is made up of several vertebrae in the cervical, thoracic and lumbar region
