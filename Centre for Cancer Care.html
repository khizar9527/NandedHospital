Centre for Cancer Care

Overview

One of the first dedicated oncology centres of India...today it is a referral centre for cancer patients from all over the world and a beacon of hope in the fight against the dark & deadly disease.

The Centre for Cancer Care at P.D. Hinduja Hospital is equipped to treat all types of adult and paediatric cancers, has different protocols and modalities for each type and a group approach for the cancers that involve many specialities.

It has always believed and strived to procure the most advanced machinery, adopt the latest medical protocols, get on board the brightest minds and hands in the field and invest in rigorous research, so that, the patients can get the best possible cancer care and stay a step ahead of the disease.

Recognizing that cancer patients need emotional support and counselling besides medicines, the hospital has also set up support groups, counsellors, social work services and palliative care services.

The centre offers holistic cancer care with the following services :
-Medical Oncology
-Radiation Oncology
-Surgical Oncology
-Palliative Care
-Patient Support Groups
