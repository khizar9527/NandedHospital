Centre for Neuro Care

The centre for neuro sciences offers a comprehensive care for brain and nervous system with the team of acclaimed neurologists, neuro surgeons, technicians with most advanced technology and procedures. The centre is referred with major and complex cases from across the globe.

The center has the following sections - Neurology - Provides non-surgical treatment of brain and nervous system. Neuro Surgery - Provides surgical treatment of brain issues and also other body parts impacted by nervous system like spine. Paediatric Neurology - Specialized treatment for brain and issues related to nervous system in kids.

Neurologists provide routine care and management to patients with nervous system disorders using non-surgical approaches, such as medications, electrical stimulation treatment and other therapies. They do not perform surgery.

Neurosurgeons on the other hand provide surgical treatment and rehabilitation of advanced complex disorders that impact any component of the nervous system, specialising in neurological and spine surgery.

The Neuro Sciences Centre also has a dedicated rehabilitation facility help patients with movement and function disorders. The centre has state-of-art of rehabilitation equipment including treadmill, cycle, ergo meter, multistation gym, neuro rehabilitation mat, staircase and ramp.
